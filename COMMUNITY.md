# The Vyzorix Community

Vyzorix is a community of engineers and enthusiasts reimagining software development for an AI-powered world.

## Mission

AI is changing software development. We want to build tools that empower developers to harness AI effectively, tailored to their own habits, needs, and imagination.

## Ethos

We have two core values: **high openness** and **high agency**.

### High Openness

We welcome anyone and everyone into our community by default. You don't have to be a software developer to help us build. You don't have to be pro-AI to help us learn.

We welcome thoughtful criticism, whether it's a comment on a PR or feedback on the community as a whole.

### High Agency

Everyone should feel empowered to contribute to Vyzorix. Whether it's by making a PR, sharing feedback, or just asking a question, don't hold back!

Vyzorix gives everyone the building blocks to create state-of-the-art developer experiences. We experiment constantly and love building new things.
